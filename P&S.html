18MAS05 - PROBABILITY AND STATISTICS
Number of credits : 4

Info

Continuous Internal Assessment I

Probability: Probability concepts-Addition and Multiplication law of probability, Conditional

probability, Total probability theorem, Bayes theorem.

Random Variables: One dimensional random variable, Probability mass function, Probability

density function.

Continuous Internal Assessment II

Standard distributions: Discrete distributions - Binomial – Poisson – Geometric, Continuous

distributions - Uniform – Exponential, Normal distributions, Simple problems.

Two dimensional random variables: Joint distributions, Marginal and conditional distributions

for Discrete Random Variable.

Continuous Internal Assessment III

Two dimensional random variables: Joint distributions, Marginal and conditional distributions

for Continuous Random Variable, Covariance, Correlation, Regression.

Testing of Hypothesis: Large sample - z test, Proportion and Mean, Small sample: t- test, F-test –

difference of means and variance, Chi-square test for goodness of fit and independence of

attributes.
